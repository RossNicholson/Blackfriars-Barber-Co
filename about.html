About us

Blackfriars Barber Co. is built on craft, consistency, and a calm chair. We serve professionals in the City who want a sharp cut and a straightforward experience—no appointments, no fuss.

Every cut is a consultation first. We take your length, shape, and style into account, then deliver a finish that holds from Monday to Friday.
